What Are Stand-Alone AI Tools?

Stand-alone AI tools are independent applications or platforms powered by artificial intelligence that work on their own without needing to be integrated into other software. They can perform tasks such as generating content, creating images, or analyzing data without requiring additional plugins or external systems. Examples include **ChatGPT** for text generation, **DALL·E** for AI image creation, **Claude** for intelligent assistance, and **MidJourney** for visual creativity.
